Authorization level

Level 1 means that the CRA may disclose the following to your

representative:

   • information given on your tax return;

   • adjustments to your tax return;

   • information about your registered retirement savings plan, Home

     Buyer's Plan, and Lifelong Learning Plan;

   • your accounting information, including balances, payments on filing,

     and instalments or transfers;

   • information about your benefits and credits (Canada Child Tax

     Benefit, goods and services tax/harmonized sales tax credit); and

   • your marital status (but not information related to your spouse or

     common-law partner).


Level 2 means that the CRA may disclose the information listed in level 1

to your representative , they may also ask for changes to your account.

Such changes include:

   • adjustments to income, deductions, and non-refundable tax credits;

   • accounting transfers.
