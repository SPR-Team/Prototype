Banking information privacy notice for individual

taxpayers and benefit recipients.

The Income Tax act allows personal information, including the social

insurance number, to be collected to administer the business and

information returns and payment processing program for the federal

government and for most provinces and territories.


Personal information is also used to determine eligibility for tax credits

and rebates, and it can be used for audit, compliance, or evaluation

purposes. It can be given to other federal, provincial, or territorial

government institutions or verified with them.


Not providing the information may result in interest payable, penalties,

or other actions. Individuals have a right to access and correct their

personal information, under the Privacy Act. Refer to Personal

Information Bank CRA PPU 005 in Info Source
