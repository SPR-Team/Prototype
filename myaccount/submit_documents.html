Help

This service allows you the opportunity to send documents electronically to the CRA. You can only use this service when:

 

   • you would like to send relevant documents that are associated to a specific program through the CRA's online

     services; or

   • we have communicated with you requesting documents, and have invited you to use this service.

 

You can use this service in the following cases:

 

   • you do not have a case or reference number but know the applicable topic for your document submission; or

   • the CRA provided you with a case or reference number.
